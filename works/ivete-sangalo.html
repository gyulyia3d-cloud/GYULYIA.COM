About the Project
3D visuals created for Ivete Sangalo's performance at Rock in Rio Lisboa — one of the biggest music festivals in the world. The project brought immersive, cinematic visuals to the main stage, amplifying the energy of one of Brazil's most celebrated artists to a global audience.
Tools & Process
3D animation and motion design developed for large-scale LED stage environment. Visual content engineered to sync with live performance, translating the artist's musical identity into dynamic visual storytelling.
