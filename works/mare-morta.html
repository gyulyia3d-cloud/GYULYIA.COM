About the Project
Maré Morta (2025) is an audiovisual installation developed in Unreal Engine, combining environmental data and generative imagery to visualize the impact of climate change on ocean ecosystems. The work transforms scientific data into an immersive poetic experience — where biology is not neutral and suffering has a class dimension.
Concept & Tools
The project was developed using Unreal Engine for real-time rendering and generative visual systems. Environmental and scientific data was translated into visual language — exploring themes of ecological collapse, ocean life cycles, and the intersection of art and environmental consciousness. Collaborators: @olavoekman, @mariliapasculli, @padreart
